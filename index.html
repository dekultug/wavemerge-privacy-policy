1. Summary

WaveMerge is an audio and video utility app for recording audio, cutting audio, merging audio files, converting audio formats, extracting audio from video, adding audio to video, removing audio from video, making ringtones, applying voice effects, downloading supported online audio links, and transcribing speech to text.

The app is designed so that core editing and transcription processing happens on your device. We do not operate a developer server that stores your personal audio files, videos, recordings, or transcripts.

2. Information We Access or Process

Media files and recordings

When you choose an audio or video file, WaveMerge accesses the selected file so it can perform the action you requested, such as trimming, merging, converting, compressing, adding audio, removing audio, previewing, sharing, or saving output files. File names, paths, duration, size, and format metadata may be used locally to display file lists and processing results.

When you use the recording feature, WaveMerge accesses your microphone and saves the resulting recording on your device only for the features you choose.

Transcripts

If you use Audio to Text, the selected audio or video is processed on your device. Transcript text and timestamp segments may be stored in the app's local database so you can view them later in the Output section. These transcripts are not uploaded to our servers.

Online links and network requests

If you paste an online audio or video link, WaveMerge may connect to the source service to fetch stream information and download the requested audio. The source service may receive information such as the link, your IP address, device/browser-like request metadata, and other information required to serve the content. Please review the privacy policy and terms of that source service.

WaveMerge may also use network access to download speech recognition model files, check connectivity, load remote configuration, process in-app purchases, app updates, app reviews, diagnostics, and related Google services.

Diagnostics, analytics, configuration, messaging, and purchases

The app includes Google Firebase and Google Play services. Depending on your device, app version, and configuration, these services may process app instance identifiers, crash reports, diagnostic data, device information, operating system version, app version, usage events, remote configuration fetches, cloud messaging tokens, purchase state, and transaction metadata. These services help us understand app stability, improve features, prevent abuse, deliver configuration, and support purchases.

3. Android Permissions Used by WaveMerge

Permission Purpose
READ_MEDIA_AUDIO Lets you select and edit audio files on Android 13 and newer.
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO Lets you select videos for features such as extracting audio, removing audio, cutting video, compressing video, adding audio to video, and transcription.
READ_MEDIA_VISUAL_USER_SELECTED Supports Android's selected-media access flow on newer Android versions.
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Lets you select media files on older Android versions. This permission is limited by the app manifest to Android 12L/API 32 and below.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Lets the app save output files on older Android versions. This permission is limited by the app manifest to Android 9/API 28 and below. On newer Android versions, WaveMerge uses scoped storage and MediaStore where applicable.
RECORD_AUDIO Lets you record audio with the microphone for the record audio feature and related editing workflows.
WRITE_SETTINGS Used only when you choose to set an exported audio file as the device's default ringtone.
INTERNET Used for online link processing, speech model downloads, Firebase/Google services, in-app purchases, app updates, and app review features.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Lets the app check whether a network connection is available before network-dependent features run.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Used on supported Android versions to show transcription progress and completion notifications.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Allows long-running processing, such as background transcription, to continue reliably with a visible ongoing notification.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC Declares the foreground service type used for data-sync style background work, such as model download and transcription progress.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide audio and video editing features requested by you.
  • To save output files, ringtones, recordings, and transcripts on your device.
  • To display recent outputs, previews, progress states, and transcript results.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not upload your selected audio files, videos, recordings, or transcript content to a developer-operated server.

Data may be processed by third-party service providers only as needed to operate app features, including:

  • Google Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, and Cloud Messaging.
  • Google Play Billing, Google Play In-App Review, and Google Play In-App Updates.
  • Online source services when you paste or use an online audio/video link.
  • Android platform services such as MediaStore, system ringtone settings, notifications, and file sharing.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request.

6. Data Retention

  • Output audio/video files, recordings, and ringtones are stored on your device until you delete them.
  • Transcripts stored in the app's local database remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the app.
  • Crash reports, analytics, remote configuration, messaging, and purchase data are retained by Google services according to their own policies and retention practices.
  • We retain support emails or messages only as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request and maintain support records.
